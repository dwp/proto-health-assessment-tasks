Prototype: Special rules for end of life (SREL assessment)

The following prototypes have been created to test designs for PIP health assessments where a claimant is terminally ill and is applying under special rules for end of life.

These designs are currently owned by team Kestrel.

If you have any questions about this prototype, or to arrange a demo, contact:

  • James Ashcroft - Interaction Designer
  • Emily Colman - Senior Content Designer
  • Annette Ek - Senior User Researcher
